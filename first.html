COURT OF JUSTICE

In the life of the citizens of a state,
Judiciary is a source of confidence and fearlessness.
The common man depends upon judiciary for getting justice.
Without a security of rights and freedom guaranteed by the judiciary,
they cannot really hope to carry out
their jobs and enjoy their living.
They are more dependent upon judiciary than the legislature and the executive.
Without judicial protection, their lives can become miserable.
From citizens point of view Judiciary is the most important organ of the government.
